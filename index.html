Mapping Citi Bike rides in 09/2020 and 09/2021


Citi Bike is the nation's largest bike sharing system, with 25,000 bikes and over 1,500 stations across Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, Jersey City and Hoboken. I visualized data for September 2020 and September 2021 in which i selected bikers start and stops across New York City. The locations of these stations, provided in the data, were converted into GEOJSON using geopandas library in python.
